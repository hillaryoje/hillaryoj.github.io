About Me

Hello! I'm Hillary Ojeikere, a passionate Computer Science student at Duke University. I'm driven by the intersection of technology, social impact, and innovation.

As a leader in organizations like NSBE and DukeAfrica, I work to bridge the gap between technology and underrepresented communities. My interests span from software engineering and AI ethics to tech policy and creative writing. I'm fluent in Yoruba, proficient in French, and always eager to learn and grow.
